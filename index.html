history of ABBA

ABBA first formed in 1969 by a group of two couples: Anni-Frid Lystad and Benny Andresson, and Bjorn and Agnetha. These Swedish made their first big appearance at eurovision-an international singing competition. Years later they had a musical revolving around thier extrodinary albums. In 2008 Mamma Mia! by Catherine Johson and Phyllida Lloyd was released along with 18 ABBA songs for each sceane.
